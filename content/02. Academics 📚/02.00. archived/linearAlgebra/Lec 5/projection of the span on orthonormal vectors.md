# projection of the span on orthonormal vectors
### definition
- $\operatorname*{min}_{x}\;f(x):=\left\|p-\sum_{i=1}^{d}u_{i}x_{i}\right\|_{2}^{2}$, $p \in R^n, x \in R^d, \{u_1, \cdots, u_d\}$ is a set of orthonormal vectors in $R^n$
